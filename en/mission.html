Our Mission

The Holy Retreat of Silence

A unique refuge where individuals facing crises can find genuine shelter and a renewed drive to move forward.

Core Goal

Establishing the Holy Retreat of Silence

The foundational and paramount objective of the Foundation is to call into existence the "Holy Retreat of Silence" — a unique refuge where individuals facing crises of faith, family, profession, or identity can find genuine shelter.

We believe that every human being deserves understanding and assistance during trying times, which is why our organization remains completely open to all. We conduct our activities in a spirit of absolute inclusivity, drawing deep inspiration from the spirituality of St. Romuald, Paul Giustiniani, and St. Vincent Pallotti.

How We Achieve Our Goal

Multi-Dimensional Support

We bring the main goal of the Foundation to life through actions that blend professional expertise with spiritual care:

🩺

Expert Specialist Assistance

Providing reliable psychological support, pedagogical counseling, and individual, marital, and group psychotherapy.

🌱

Natural Therapies

Utilizing innovative methods such as animalotherapy (working with horses and alpacas) and hortitherapy (healing through contact with the garden).

🙏

Stillness and Formation

Organizing retreats, running a school of Christian meditation, and teaching biblical catechesis to create an environment perfect for regaining inner peace.

The Setting

A Space That Heals Through Silence

Our main headquarters is located in a historic manor house from the mid-19th century. Its classic structure, boasting a volume of over 2,500 m³, gracefully overlooks the picturesque landscape. The manor is embraced by an expansive estate of over two hectares, intentionally designed as a sanctuary for spiritual and physical restoration.

  • The Park and the Lake: Direct proximity to the lake and serene parkland allow our guests to breathe deeply and completely unplug from urban bustle.
  • Harmony with Architecture: A rustic therapeutic stable and an alpaca sanctuary are seamlessly integrated into the landscape to preserve historical charm.
  • Sensory Pathways: Specially designed sensory stations featuring sand, bark, and smooth river stones allow for authentic grounding during animal-assisted sessions.

Our Hermitages

Returning to Eremitic Roots

For individuals seeking absolute solitude and deep convalescence of the soul, the Foundation is developing a pioneering initiative to build individual cabins — our "Hermitages."

In alignment with the traditions of St. Romuald and the Desert Fathers, we are constructing these cabins far away from crowded areas, ensuring that your only companion is the silence and the natural surroundings.

  • Secluded Locations: Intentionally selected quiet areas guaranteeing absolute stillness and intimate self-reflection.
  • Short or Long-Term Stays: Designed for eremitic stays allowing individuals to discover renewed motivation in total privacy, discretion, and peace.

Spiritual Inspiration

The Pillars of Our Faith

St. Romuald & Paul Giustiniani

The Camaldolese tradition promotes a life of solitude, silence, and deep communion with God through nature. From these roots our "Hermitages" project is born.

St. Vincent Pallotti

The patron of the Catholic Apostolate, whose teachings emphasize God's infinite mercy and the universal calling of every human being to serve their neighbor.

School of Christian Meditation

Meditation and prayer groups, biblical catechesis, and spiritual retreats helping participants discover daily calmness and stress relief.

The Retreat of Silence Is Open to Everyone

Every endeavor is purposefully directed toward promoting health, protecting families, and serving individuals facing the most challenging circumstances in life.
