Therapeutic Care

What We Offer

As part of our statutory work, we provide a range of professional therapeutic services:

👤

Individual Psychotherapy

Working through personal crises, deep traumas, and identity struggles in a safe, confidential environment.

👨‍👩‍👧

Marital & Family Therapy

Support in rebuilding relationships, improving communication, and resolving conflicts within the family unit.

👥

Group Therapy

A safe, supportive environment to share experiences and search for collective solutions among peers or specific focus groups.

Psychological & Pedagogical Counseling

Professional guidance for current parenting, behavioral, and educational challenges. Tailored support designed around your specific situation.

Qualifications & Expertise

Led by Fr. Jarosław Leszek Dąbrowski

The primary pillar of our specialist care is our Founder, who holds specialized degrees in psychological counseling and psychotherapy from the prestigious John Paul II Catholic University of Lublin (KUL).

By blending professional psychological expertise with decades of pastoral experience, we offer support that is:

  • Regular and Systematic: Ensuring strict continuity of care, which is vital for a successful therapeutic outcome.
  • Individualized: Every support program is custom-designed, taking into account the unique life situation and personal needs of the individual.
  • Without Barriers: True to our mission, our doors remain open to anyone and everyone in need of healing.

Beyond the Consultation Room

A Holistic Framework for Health

Psychological care at the "Retreat of Silence" does not end in the consultation room. It forms an integral part of a much broader healing process, which we actively enhance through:

  • Animalotherapy and Hortitherapy: Direct contact with animals and nature serves as a powerful complement to traditional therapeutic practices.
  • Tranquility and Spirituality: Leveraging the historic quietude of the manor house in Wierzchucinek and Christian meditation as proven tools for stress reduction and restoring peace of mind.

Our Doors Are Open

If you feel that you need a professional, confidential conversation and reliable support to guide you through a difficult season — step into tranquility and allow us to help you.
