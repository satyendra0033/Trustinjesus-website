Plans & Projects

Building a Haven for Body and Soul

Transforming a historic estate into a sanctuary where history meets nature, and professional help meets deep stillness.

The Heart of Our Plans

A Second Life for the Historic Manor House

Within the historic, spacious walls of our 19th-century manor house, we are establishing a professional center for psychotherapy and education.

We are meticulously overseeing every detail to ensure that this venue — from its foundations to its roofs — exudes peace.

Animals & Nature

A Gentle Realm

We believe that connecting with nature is the shortest path to healing, which is why we are cultivating a unique therapeutic zone right next to the manor house:

🐴

A Light-Filled Stable

A beautiful home for our horses in a warm, rustic architectural style, designed to allow animals to roam freely onto green pastures — happy, healthy, and ready for animalotherapy sessions.

👣

The Garden of Senses

Imagine walking barefoot along a winding pathway carpeted with soft moss, fragrant tree bark, and smooth river stones. Our sensory path will reconnect you with the earth and discover mindfulness in every step.

🦙

The Alpaca Sanctuary

Nestled in the quietude of our park, a cozy haven for alpacas — gentle creatures whose calm demeanor has the power to soothe the deepest sorrows.

Total Disconnect

"Hermitages" — Your Very Own Desert of Peace

For those who require a total disconnect from the outside world, we are actively developing our "Hermitages" project. These are small, standalone cabins situated in serene locations far from the hustle of human settlements.

Inspired by ancient eremitic traditions, we want to provide you with a space where your only companions are the gentle rustle of the trees and your own thoughts. The perfect conditions to find answers to life's most profound questions in complete silence, safety, and absolute discretion.

Community & Growth

Spaces for Creative Growth

We are transforming our old outbuildings into vibrant creative workshops and rehabilitation halls — spaces where guests can discover hidden talents, learn new practical skills, and reclaim confidence through the work of their own hands.

Let Us Meet by the Lake

Our estate lands along the beautiful lakefront are a place of joy, community, and celebration — where we organize community integration events, bridging generations and promoting a healthy lifestyle surrounded by breathtaking nature.

Together, We Are Building Hope

Every step we take in developing the "Retreat of Silence" is taken with you in mind. Together, we are building a place where the darkness of a crisis is transformed into the light of a new hope.
