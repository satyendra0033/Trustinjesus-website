Our Mission

The Holy Retreat of Silence

The primary mission of the Foundation is to establish the "Holy Retreat of Silence" — a sanctuary dedicated to providing comprehensive, holistic support to individuals navigating family, professional, and identity crises, as well as anyone seeking renewed purpose and motivation in life.

The Foundation warmly embraces every human being, regardless of their background, grounding its work in the profound Christian spiritual traditions of St. Romuald, Paul Giustiniani, and St. Vincent Pallotti.

What We Offer

Embracing Tranquility: Your Path to Inner Balance

Peace is not merely the absence of noise — it is a state of the heart where you rediscover the strength to face life's challenges.

🕊️

The Silence That Heals

Our center in Wierzchucinek is the "Holy Retreat of Silence." The rhythm of the day is dictated by nature and the spirituality of St. Romuald. Silence becomes a profound tool for mental and spiritual regeneration.

🧘

Christian Meditation

We host regular Christian meditation sessions and biblical catechesis designed to quiet racing thoughts, helping you reconnect with yourself and with God.

💬

Professional Support

We offer expert psychological and psychotherapeutic assistance. Under the guidance of Fr. Jarosław Dąbrowski, a specialist in psychological counseling, we help you process family, professional, or identity struggles.

🐴

Animalotherapy

Interacting with our animals — horses and alpacas — effectively reduces stress and restores trust in the world around us.

🌿

Hortitherapy

Working in our therapeutic garden provides healing through direct contact with nature, engaging all your senses.

🏡

Hermitages

Individual cabins in secluded locations, inspired by the Desert Fathers, for those seeking absolute solitude and deep soul convalescence.

The Founder

Jarosław Leszek Dąbrowski

A spiritual guide and the visionary founder of the Holy Retreat of Silence. Guided by a life of contemplation, humility, and service, he has dedicated his work to creating a space where individuals can withdraw from the noise of everyday life and rediscover inner peace, clarity, and spiritual renewal.

He holds specialized degrees in psychological counseling and psychotherapy from the John Paul II Catholic University of Lublin (KUL), blending professional expertise with decades of pastoral experience.

Our Vision

A Haven of Hope and Renewal

We envision a future where every individual facing a crisis can find a safe harbor to reclaim their inner balance and a new motivation to live.

A Space Filled with Peace

Complete revitalization of the historic 19th-century manor house in Wierzchucinek and its surrounding parkland — over 2,500 m³ of classic architecture harmonizing with modern therapy and spiritual growth.

Holistic Therapy

A professional psychotherapy and counseling center led by qualified experts, enhanced by innovative animal-assisted therapy and hortitherapy. Nature combined with professional support is the key to healing.

Living Spirituality

A thriving center for Christian meditation, retreats, and biblical studies, drawing from the rich traditions of St. Romuald and St. Vincent Pallotti — open to all, regardless of background or status.

Take the First Step

Whether you are looking for a single day of quiet reflection, an extended stay, or professional therapy — the Retreat of Silence is open to everyone, without exception.
