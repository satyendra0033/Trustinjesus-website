How You Can Help

Your Support Builds the Retreat of Silence

Every contribution becomes a vital building block for a sanctuary filled with peace and hope.

Ways to Give

Choose How You Can Help

1. Donate 1.5% of Your Tax

As an officially registered Public Benefit Organization (OPP) in Poland, we offer you the opportunity to decide exactly where your tax money goes. By allocating 1.5% of your tax to our Foundation, you directly fund free psychological counseling, animal-assisted therapies, and the essential maintenance of the Holy Retreat of Silence.

Our KRS Number: 0000998367

2. Financial Donations: Become a Patron

Your financial support allows us to secure ongoing therapy sessions for those in need and directly funds the restoration of our historic manor house.

  • Donor Title: Awarded to individuals and institutions who regularly support our mission with monthly or periodic contributions.
  • Honorary Patron of the Foundation (Honorowy Mecenas Fundacji): This prestigious title is bestowed upon individuals and corporate entities whose single or cumulative donations reach a minimum of 15,000 PLN.

3. Targeted Donations for Specific Projects

You have the power to choose exactly which element of the Retreat of Silence you wish to fund:

  • Therapeutic Stable & Alpaca Sanctuary: Help us build a loving home for our animal co-therapists.
  • Manor House Revitalization: Support the physical restoration of historic interiors destined to become therapy and counseling rooms.
  • The "Hermitages" Project: Your donation can directly sponsor the construction of a new secluded cabin for individuals in need of radical quietude and privacy.

4. Bequests, Legacies, and In-Kind Donations

The Foundation is legally authorized to accept in-kind donations, specific bequests, and inherited legacies. Whether you wish to donate interior equipment for the manor house, building materials, or support us through other material means — every form of help is profoundly valuable.

Why Stand With Us?

Full Transparency

The Foundation is fully accountable for its obligations with its entire estate, and our operations are strictly supervised by the Minister of Family and Social Policy.

This stringent state oversight guarantees absolute financial transparency, integrity, and accountability in the management of all entrusted funds.

Spiritual Benefit

All donors are warmly invited into the spiritual life of our community. This includes dedicated, regular priestly prayers offered by our clergy for your personal intentions and the well-being of your loved ones.

Thank You for Standing With Us
